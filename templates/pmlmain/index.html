    Our Humble Beginnings

    Progress Men's League (PML) is a community-based, development-oriented organisation, founded in 1991 It is comprised of men brought together by common vision and purpose.

  • Membership

    Men of Integrity with a Vision

    Our current membership stands at 34. A the moment membership is closed and new recruits may be considered in the future.

    Administrative and Executive Functions

    A democratically elected Executive Committee performs administrative and executive functions. The Executive Committee has a constitutional mandate to establish sub-committees. The Constitution is the source of authority and policy of the League
